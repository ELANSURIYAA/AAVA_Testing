### **1. Domain Overview**

The data model represents a "Person" domain, which is a common requirement in healthcare and other industries for managing patient or customer information. The model is designed to store demographic, contact, and language information for each person. The data is sourced from various systems and includes timestamps for tracking data lineage.

### **2. List of Entity Names with descriptions**

1.  **Person**: This is the main entity representing an individual. It contains demographic, insurance, and other personal information.
2.  **ContactInfo**: This entity stores the contact details of a person, including their address, phone number, and email.
3.  **Language**: This entity stores the spoken and written language preferences of a person.

### **3. List of Attributes for each Entity with descriptions for each attribute**

#### **Person Entity**

*   **PersonID** (Primary Key): A unique identifier for the person.
*   **DOB**: The date of birth of the person.
*   **Address**: The current address of the person.
*   **ContactNumber**: The contact number of the person.
*   **EmailId**: The email address of the person.
*   **Gender**: The gender of the person.
*   **Race**: The racial identity of the person.
*   **Ethnicity**: The ethnic background of the person.
*   **Spoken**: The primary spoken language of the person.
*   **Written**: The primary written language of the person.
*   **Insurance**: The health insurance provider company name.
*   **Provider**: The primary healthcare provider of the person.
*   **SourceSystem**: The system where the data originated.
*   **IngestionTimestamp**: The timestamp when the record was ingested.
*   **MaritalStatus**: The marital status of the person.
*   **EmploymentStatus**: The current employment status of the person.
*   **EducationLevel**: The highest level of education attained by the person.
*   **Nationality**: The nationality of the person.
*   **EmergencyContact**: The emergency contact details of the person.
*   **LastUpdated**: The last modification timestamp of the record.

#### **ContactInfo Entity**

*   **PersonID** (Foreign Key): A unique identifier for the person, referencing the Person entity.
*   **Address**: The current address of the person.
*   **Phone**: The phone number of the person.
*   **Email**: The email address of the person.

#### **Language Entity**

*   **PersonID** (Foreign Key): A unique identifier for the person, referencing the Person entity.
*   **Spoken**: The primary spoken language of the person.
*   **Written**: The primary written language of the person.
