### **1. Domain Overview**

The conceptual data model represents member data for an organization. It captures essential information about each member, including their personal details, membership status, and engagement activities. This model is designed to support various business functions, such as member management, targeted marketing, and service personalization.

### **2. List of Entity Names with Descriptions**

1.  **Member**: The primary entity representing an individual member of the organization. It serves as the central hub for all member-related information.
2.  **PersonalInfo**: A sub-entity containing the member’s personal identification details. This entity is directly related to the Member entity.
3.  **Contact**: A sub-entity that stores the member’s contact information. It is linked to the Member entity.
4.  **Membership**: A sub-entity that holds details about the member’s subscription, including tier, duration, and status.
5.  **ActivityLog**: A sub-entity that records member interactions and activities, providing insights into their engagement.

### **3. List of Attributes for each Entity with Descriptions**

#### **1. Member Entity**

*   **memberId (Primary Key)**: A unique identifier assigned to each member.
*   **firstName**: The first name of the member.
*   **lastName**: The last name of the member.
*   **dateOfBirth**: The member's date of birth.
*   **email**: The member's primary email address.
*   **phone**: The member's phone number.
*   **tier**: The level of membership (e.g., Gold, Silver).
*   **startDate**: The date when the membership commenced.
*   **renewalDate**: The date when the membership is due for renewal.
*   **status**: The current status of the membership (e.g., Active, Inactive).

#### **2. PersonalInfo Entity**

*   **memberId (Foreign Key)**: A reference to the Member entity, linking personal info to a specific member.
*   **firstName**: The first name of the member.
*   **lastName**: The last name of the member.
*   **dateOfBirth**: The member's date of birth.

#### **3. Contact Entity**

*   **memberId (Foreign Key)**: A reference to the Member entity.
*   **email**: The member's email address.
*   **phone**: The member's phone number.

#### **4. Membership Entity**

*   **memberId (Foreign Key)**: A reference to the Member entity.
*   **tier**: The membership tier.
*   **startDate**: The start date of the membership.
*   **renewalDate**: The renewal date of the membership.
*   **status**: The current status of the membership.

#### **5. ActivityLog Entity**

*   **activityId (Primary Key)**: A unique identifier for each activity log entry.
*   **memberId (Foreign Key)**: A reference to the Member entity.
*   **date**: The date of the activity.
*   **description**: A brief description of the activity.

### **5. List of Constraints**

1.  **Uniqueness**: `memberId` and `email` must be unique for each member.
2.  **Data Integrity**: `dateOfBirth` must be a date in the past. `renewalDate` must be after `startDate`.
3.  **Domain Integrity**: `tier` and `status` attributes must conform to their predefined categorical values.
4.  **Referential Integrity**: The `memberId` in `PersonalInfo`, `Contact`, `Membership`, and `ActivityLog` must exist in the `Member` entity.
