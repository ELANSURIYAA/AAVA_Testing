## Detailed Analysis

### Semantic Similarity (Score: 85/100)

Both outputs address identical member data validation objectives with highly aligned intent. Agent 2 expands on Agent 1's foundation with additional granular rules (string length checks for FIRSTNAME/LASTNAME, enhanced format validations). Minor semantic differences in rule naming (ValueCheck vs DomainCheck, MinimumRequirementCheck vs ContactInfoPresenceCheck) but identical underlying logic.

**Key Alignments:**
- Both enforce FIRSTNAME and LASTNAME not-null requirements (High severity)
- Both validate CITY against approved locations: Los Angeles, New York, London
- Both ensure MEMBER_ID uniqueness and not-null constraints
- Both require at least one contact method (PHONENUMBER1, EMAIL, or WEB)
- Both validate email and web URL formats

**Differences:**
