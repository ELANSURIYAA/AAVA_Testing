## Key Observations

- **Overlapping Tables:** Most business domains (customer, employee, order, transaction, campaign) are represented in all three layers, though table and column names are prefixed differently.
- **Overlapping Columns:** Core business attributes (e.g., customer_id, email, campaign_id, etc.) are present in all layers, but data types and lengths may differ.
- **Unique Tables:** Some tables are unique to a specific layer, reflecting specialized use cases or landing/staging requirements.
- **Unique Columns:** Most columns are shared, but some columns (e.g., in campaign_number_marketing or order_product_quantity/order_product_date) are unique to their respective layers.
- **Data Type/Length Differences:** There are notable differences in data types (e.g., INT vs. VARCHAR, VARCHAR length, DECIMAL precision) and some columns are missing in certain layers.
